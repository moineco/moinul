Moinul

I am Dr. Moinul Islam, working as an assistant professor at Kochi University of Technology. I have received my Ph.D. in engineering degree from Kyushu University in 2016. My laboratory focuses on development economics, experimental economics, environmental economics, and spacial economics.

The research method focuses on the following three points.

  1. Development Economics
  2. Experimental Economics
  3. Environmental Economics
  4. Spatial Economics

I have extensive experience to conduct experimental research by using the methods of online experiment, field experiment, laboratory experiment and spatial econometrics. I use computer programming (Python), data analysis software (R, oTree) and geographic information system (GIS) for experimental research design. Based on the experimental data analysis, my goal is to identify and develop suitable policy framework that will contribute to the policies for sustainable environments and societies.
